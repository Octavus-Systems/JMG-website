02  /  Work

Case study

PureSteel Construction Inc. · Dallas, TX

From subcontractor onboarding to signed document filing, end to end.

PureSteel Construction Inc. is a commercial steel contractor based in Dallas managing multiple active projects simultaneously. The owner was handling subcontract agreements, change orders, and subcontractor onboarding entirely by hand. Every document drafted manually, every signature chased over email, every signed PDF filed by hand.

I designed and built a suite of interconnected automation workflows, end to end, from subcontractor onboarding to signed document filing. The systems are live, actively used, and maintained by one person.

10+ hours of manual administrative work eliminated per month
